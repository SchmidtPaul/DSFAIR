Data

This example is taken from Chapter “4.1 The sorghum experiment” of the course material “Mixed models for metric data (3402-451)” by Prof. Dr. Hans-Peter Piepho. It considers data published in Piepho (1997) from a greenhouse experiment with sorghum, three intensities of double use of grain and leaves were tested: (1) control (grain only) Ctrl; (2) removal of all leaves except top leaf Top1; (3) removal of all leaves except top six leaves Top6.
